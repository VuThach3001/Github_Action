### Notes Section (Main Notes)

**1. Understanding Job Outputs**

**Artifacts**
- Output files & folders: Typically used for sharing log files, app binaries etc.

**Job Outputs:**
- Simple values: Typically used for re-using a value in different jobs
- E.g: Name of a file generated in a previous build step
